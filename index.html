Phewa Lake lies beside Pokhara in western Nepal, reflecting the Annapurna range across calm waters, offering boating, lakeside temples, culture, peaceful scenery, and stunning sunsets that draw travelers seeking relaxation and adventure.

Dhorpatan

📍Dhorpatan,Nepal

Dhorpatan lies in western Nepal across Baglung, Myagdi, and Rukum districts, offering alpine meadows, rugged valleys, and the country’s only hunting reserve, renowned for diverse wildlife, highland culture, and pristine mountain landscapes.
